Stun at every angle
A 360° design philosophy puts subtle, but distinctive elements across every part of the machine. For our Original Black and Eclipse Gray variants, a dot matrix design evokes an illuminated cityscape with a clean and professional polish. Bolder Electro Punk looks have an angled cybertext pattern across the keyboard for futuristic flair. Even the bottom of these laptops aren’t forgotten, adorned with cross-hatched grip beneath the base and typographic elements around the trim that show off ROG insignia.
AMD® Ryzen™ CPUs
Power through intensive tasks with up to the latest AMD Ryzen™ 9 5900HX CPU. Built using cutting-edge 7nm fabrication technology, this 8-core processor accelerates demanding workflows, activating up to 16 parallel threads for faster gaming and beyond. Stream your favorite channels, jump into multiplayer, and stay on top of your task list anywhere. Bolstered by Intelligent Cooling, the unlocked CPU can even be overclocked for faster performance.
NVIDIA® GeForce® RTX™ graphics
Step up your gaming graphics with a new, leading-edge NVIDIA RTX GPU. The latest XX architecture’s mix of CUDA, RT, and Tensor cores blends programmable shading, ray tracing, and artificial intelligence in real time to enhance in-game lighting and effects, from realistic reflections to shifting shadows. This potent processing power can also be harnessed to accelerate a range of other workloads, from content creation to 3D modeling to deep learning.
Dual-channel DDR4 memory
Multitask faster with up to 32GB of dual-channel DDR4-3200 memory. Gamers can switch gears between the latest AAA titles, stream, and chat with teammates without missing a beat, while serious power users and creators can run every program they need at once for peak productivity.
Superfast SSD storage
Take back time for tasks that matter with superfast SSD storage. Two responsive M.2 NVMe PCIe® 3.0 SSDs make massive games and apps load at blazing fast speeds, boosting your productivity. Up to 1TB of storage gives you the space for your favorite games and entertainment experiences.
Liquid metal
Liquid metal compound from Thermal Grizzly reduces CPU temperatures by up to 12°C compared to standard thermal paste. Painting liquid metal onto CPUs is delicate work typically done by hand, but ROG commissioned custom equipment to automate the process with mechanical precision. The block that sits on the CPU has a patented internal fence that prevents liquid metal from leaking out over time.
New n-Blade fans
Upgraded n-Blade fans have 84 blades shaped to draw in more air with less noise. Each ultrathin fan blade is placed to create an uneven outer edge that minimizes noise caused by turbulence without diminishing airflow. Once inside, the air is blasted toward the heatsinks by blades that are 33% thinner than conventional designs yet strong enough to spin at high RPMs. The fans channel warm air out of the chassis through four fan outlets, speeding up heat dissipation.
More ultrathin fins
Thermal energy is dissipated by heatsinks in the flank and channeled out through 4 fan outlets. Each heatsink is lined with ultra-thin copper fins as slim as 0.1mm, allowing higher density and lower air resistance than typical fins that are twice as thick. Depending on the CPU and GPU, there are up to 284 heatsink fins totaling 178,125mm2 of surface area for rapid dissipation.
Lower noise levels
A new cooling design keeps acoustics quieter for work and play. The combination of improved n-Blade fans, more fan outlets, and liquid metal compound that lowers CPU temperatures, we can boost performance while minimizing noise. Silent mode stays at a whisper under 35 dB for less intensive tasks, while Performance and Turbo modes offer serious gaming performance with 40-45 dB of sound.
3D Flow Zone
Ample airflow is a fundamental aspect of effective cooling. To amp up cool air intake for the Strix G15/17, its hinges are shifted forward in front of a heavily ventilated 3D Flow Zone. A trapezoidal cut carves out the bottom of the screen to ensure that the venting below is unobstructed, while giving the laptop its signature asymmetrical style.
CoolZone keyboard
Extended gaming sessions can heat up internal components and make keyboards warm to the touch. To keep your fingers cool, small vents are cut across the keyboard that let the fans below draw cool air down into the chassis. Surface temperatures are reduced by up to 2° Celcius, keeping the keyboard comfortable to use.
Self-cleaning cooling system
Keeping the heatsinks free of debris is critical to maintaining optimal thermal dissipation, so our engineers designed a special anti-dust tunnel that catches and channels particles outside the chassis to prevent them from collecting on the fins. This self-cleaning system helps ensure consistent cooling performance over time, which improves long-term stability and overall reliability.
Extended heat spreader
The comprehensive thermal design includes an extended heat spreader that absorbs heat from the GPU and CPU and redistributes it to minimize thermal throttling. The heat spreader stretches across up to 6 heat pipes, allowing it to efficiently keep power components below 85° Celsius. This consistent thermal management helps sustain and extend the overall performance, reliability, and lifespan of your system.
Keyboard layout
Taking inspiration from the best desktop gaming keyboards, the Strix G15/17 features function keys that are spaced for fewer misses, and directional arrows that are isolated for precise fingertip control. The Space bar is extended and reshaped for greater comfort and accuracy when you need to jump into the thick of battle. Our 15” devices feature a digital numpad on the touchpad available at the press of a button, while 17” versions offer a full numeric keypad.
Dedicated hotkeys
Dedicated gaming hotkeys sit isolated above the keyboard for quick access to the volume and mic mute controls, operating modes, and Armoury Crate. They’re positioned on the upper left side, so you can access them instantly without tabbing out or taking your right hand off the mouse. Switch on your mic to strategize with your team or turn up the volume to hear every enemy movement in your vicinity without losing focus on the game.
Overstroke technology
With exclusive ROG Overstroke technology, keypresses are registered earlier in the stroke, enabling a more responsive typing experience for serious gaming and work. Early key actuation means faster, effortless input that can improve your accuracy over time, while the longer post-actuation travel distance allows for smoother control and comfort. Increase your actions per minute with a keyboard calibrated for precision gaming.
Immersive sound
Richer audio draws you more deeply into your gameplay and media. Virtual 5.1.2-channel surround sound powered by Dolby Atmos enhances your spatial immersion with more nuanced, layered sound effects that can be further optimized with specially tuned preset modes for movies and games.
AI Noise Cancelation
Our advanced Two-Way AI Noise Cancelation technology runs on the CPU, which frees up the GPU to hit higher FPS. It works for any upstream device and also for downstream connections, letting you remove noise from both your own mic input and from incoming feeds. Noise-canceling settings can even be customized per program, filtering specific apps like voice chat without affecting game audio you want to remain untouched.
